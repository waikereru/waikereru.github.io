Cyclone Vainau paid an unwelcome visit to Waikereru in April, and knocked over some old titoki and 20 year old kanuka trees like ninepins. Tim saw their canopies whirling in the wind before they crashed - very sobering. Thanks so much to the Tindall Foundation for giving us a generous grant to sort out the damage, and to support the 1769 Seed Archive.
